AI Remediation is an AI-based IT service management (ITSM) infrastructure platform tailored toward UK enterprise and Managed Service Provider (MSP) industries. It targets the "Orchestration Gap" where modern IT support is still dominated by manual monitoring, ticket black-box opacity, and fragmented logs leading to system-wide blindness.

Pilot deployments showed a 42% MTTR reduction and 30% Level-1 ticket volume reduction.

Three Innovation Pillars

From Conversational Help to Evidence-Gated Remediation

Unlike ITSM tools that focus on ticket deflection via chat, AI Remediation is a controlled execution layer that independently verifies the safety of every action before performing it. Evidence must prove the fix, not just suggest it.

The Decoupling of Confidence and Permission

A novel logic where AI confidence in a solution ("I am 99% sure this is the fix") is secondary to Evidence-Gate clearance ("Do I have logs and policy permission to act?"). This prevents all hallucinated resolutions from reaching live systems.

Governance-First Orchestration The Trust Layer

Evidence Gate Opens Multi-Source Triangulation

The EGREE evidence gate simultaneously queries 3 or more independent data sources to verify the incident ground truth. No single source is trusted alone. This multi-source triangulation is what eliminates the hallucination risk that plagues traditional AI ITSM tools the AI cannot act on a belief, only on verified evidence.

CMDB check:Is the configuration state what it should be? Is this a privileged system?
Telemetry check:Does the live system data corroborate the reported incident?
Identity logs:Are there anomalous login patterns, unusual source IPs, or Tor exit nodes?
Policy database:What actions are pre-approved for this resource type? Is a change window active?
Pilot result:False positive rate below 1% industry benchmark is 15–20%

This prevents all hallucination-driven actions regardless of model quality.
